Assassin's Creed Valhalla

Assassin's Creed Valhalla is a historical action role-playing game set in 9th century England. Players take on the role of Eivor, a Viking raider, as they lead their clan to settle in the British Isles. Explore a vast, open-world environment, engage in intense battles, and uncover the mysteries of ancient England.
